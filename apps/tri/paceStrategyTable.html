  • Rack bike quickly.
  • Change into running gear efficiently.

  • Maintain even pacing.
  • Adjust pace as needed for final miles.

Nutrition and Hydration Strategy

Discipline Nutrition Strategy
Swim Hydrate before start, take a gel 15 mins before start.
Bike Consume 200-300 calories/hour from solid/liquid sources, drink regularly.
Run Consume 150-250 calories/hour, focus on gels/chews, drink at every aid station.

Mental Strategy

  • Stay Focused: Break each discipline into manageable chunks.
  • Positive Self-Talk: Use positive reinforcement during tough moments.
  • Adapt: Adjust your plan based on how you feel and race conditions.
